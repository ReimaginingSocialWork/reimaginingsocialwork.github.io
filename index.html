Reimagining Social Work

Blog Archive - Preserved November 2025

Welcome to the archived version of the Reimagining Social Work in Aotearoa blog.

The blog was operational for ten years between 2015 and 2025 and led by the Reimagining Social Work collective, a collective of social work academics based in Aotearoa New Zealand.

During its life the blog had an international audience with almost 200,000 unique vistors, 1,100 subscribers, and posted 323 blog posts on progressive social work policy and practice.

This complete archive preserves all posts, images, and functionality exactly as they appeared, captured using Browsertrix web archiving technology.

How to Find Archived Pages

If you have an old link to the blog that no longer works (e.g., from a citation, bookmark, or external website), you can find the archived version by following these steps:

  1. Copy the broken URL - This might look like:
    Old/Broken URL:
    https://www.reimaginingsocialwork.nz/2017/05/post-title/
  2. Convert to the archive format - Change the URL to use reimaginingsocialwork.nz (remove www. if present):
    Converted URL:
    https://reimaginingsocialwork.nz/2017/05/14/post-title/
  3. Search in the archive - Open the archive browser and copy your converted URL into the search box
  4. Copy the working archive link - Once the page loads in the archive, click in your browser's address bar at the top of the screen, select all the text (Cmd+A on Mac, Ctrl+A on Windows), and copy it (Cmd+C or Ctrl+C). This is the permanent link to the archived page:
